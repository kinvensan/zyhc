Powerball Jackpot – Powerball rollover

The American Powerball lottery is one of the most popular lotteries in the world, thanks to its biggest rollovers, which can bring a real fortune to the lucky winner.  The biggest jackpot in Powerball was hit in 2016 and gave the winner an amazing $1,5 billion!

Powerball rollover has exceeded $500 million dollars three times already: $587,5 million in 2012, $590,5 million in 2013, and $564,1 million in 2015.

The minimum Powerball jackpot is $40 million!
