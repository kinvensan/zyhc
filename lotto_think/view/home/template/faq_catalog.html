Is Lottopark the organiser of the offered lotteries?

No. Each of the lotteries is organised by a company eligible to organise lotteries in the area of local availability. LottoPark offers a courier service, allowing the user to buy lottery tickets offered on the website. Our goal is to help with playing lotto in an easy, convenient, and safe way.

What kind of experience in the lotto industry does LottoPark have?

The founders of LottoPark have vast experience in the lotto industry, gained by cooperating with the leading lottery organisers in Europe and the United States. The LottoPark team is a set of highly-qualified employees for whom lotteries are more than only a dayjob but a passion, which helps us provide the best online lotto experience for our users.

Where is the LottoPark headquarters located?

Lottopark headquarters is in Curacao, Netherlands Antilles. We also have a local office in London, UK.
